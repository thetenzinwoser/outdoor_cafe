## How to Use This Proposal

### Option 1: Print as PDF (Recommended)

**On Mac:**
1. Open `retainer-proposal.html` in your browser (Chrome or Safari)
2. Press `Cmd + P` to print
3. Select "Save as PDF" as the destination
4. Save the PDF to share or print

**On Windows:**
1. Open `retainer-proposal.html` in your browser
2. Press `Ctrl + P` to print
3. Select "Save as PDF" or "Microsoft Print to PDF"
4. Save the PDF

### Option 2: Share the HTML directly

You can open the HTML file in your browser and show it directly on your phone/laptop during the meeting.

### Option 3: Email the PDF

After converting to PDF, you can email it as a professional attachment.

2. **Print it** - Having a physical copy shows you're serious and professional
3. **Leave it behind** - Even if they say yes immediately, leave them the PDF to share with partners
